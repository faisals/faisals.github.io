Namaazi Support

Help and contact information for the Namaazi app

Need help with Namaazi?
Email: namaazi@faisals.net

What to include in your message

  • Device (iPhone/iPad) + iOS version
  • App version (e.g., 1.4)
  • What you expected vs what happened
  • Screenshots if relevant

Common questions

  1. The app isn’t working / is stuck
    Try closing the app and reopening it. If it persists, restart your device.

  2. Notifications aren’t working (if you have them)
    Check iOS Settings → Notifications → Namaazi and ensure notifications are enabled.

  3. Feature request / feedback
    Send suggestions to the email above—short is fine, memes accepted.

Privacy & data

Namaazi support requests are handled via email. If you’d like to request data deletion or have privacy questions, email namaazi@faisals.net with subject: "Namaazi - Privacy Request".

Response time

We typically respond within 2-3 business days.
